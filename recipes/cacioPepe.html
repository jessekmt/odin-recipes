CACIO E PEPE

Description

This is a recipe compromised of cheese, pepper, egg yolks, spaghetti(or any pasta for that matter)

Super simple to make, with minimal ingredients and effort. A classic dish

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti (or similar pasta)
  • 5 tbsp crushed pepper corns
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 50g Pecorino Romano

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to packet instructions, or till al dente. Reserve some of the pasta water
  2. Add crushed pepper corns to pan and toast for 5 mins. Add ladle of pasta water to the pan
  3. Add a little of the pasta water to the cheese and mix until a smooth paste forms. Add sprinkle of pepper.
  4. Add the Spaghetti to the pepper/water mix, cook for 4-5 mins on low. Turn off heat
  5. Spoon the cheese mixture into the pepper/water mix, mix well and serve. Sprinkle with pepper and cheese.
