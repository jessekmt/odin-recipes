LASAGNA

Description

This is a cheesy, rich and succulent Lasagna recipe. 550 Calories per portion.

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Onion
  • 4 cloves of Garlic
  • 2 Shredded Carrots
  • 1 x 400g Beef Mince
  • 2 x 400g Cans Chopped Tomatoes
  • 150ml Beef Stock
  • 4 x 30cm Pasta Sheets
  • 3 cups cheddar cheese
  1. 1. Fry off Beef till Brown
  2. Add chopped onions, garlic and carrots
  3. Add chopped Tomato Cans along with beef stock
  4. Simmer for 30 mins covered
  5. Layer baking sheet with oil, then equal amounts of the beef mixture and pasta sheets
  6. Cook for 30 mins and top with the cheese for last 10 minutes
